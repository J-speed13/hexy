A1 Learning Tips

Modern Greek grammar relies on grammatical genders (Masculine, Feminine, Neuter). Check the endings of articles and nouns to determine their category. For pronunciation, pay attention to the accent mark (τόνος); it tells you exactly where to stress the word!

Accented Stress

The stress mark (e.g., ά, έ) tells you which syllable to read louder.

The Neuter Rule

Nouns ending in -ι, -ο, or -μα are usually Neuter (το).

Politeness (Plural)

Use second-person plural (-ετε) to show formal respect to strangers.
