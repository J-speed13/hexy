Science Exam Insights

To excel in your Year 7 Science Exam, pay close attention to structure-function relationships in biology (e.g. how cell structures match their duties) and quantitative properties in chemistry (e.g. interpreting pH values and color indicators precisely).

Litmus Memory Trick

Remember: Acid turns Litmus Red (AR), and Base/Alkali turns it Blue (BB).

Cellulose Wall

Only plant cells have a cell wall made of cellulose to maintain stiff structure.

Indicator pH

pH 1 is a strong acid, pH 7 is neutral, and pH 14 is a strong alkali.
