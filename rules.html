HEXY

How to Play

1

Place Blocks

Drag and drop the given hexagonal puzzle pieces from the tray onto the main hexagonal grid.

2

Clear Lines

Create full solid lines across the board in any of the 3 primary directions to shatter the blocks and free up space.

3

Score Combos

Clear multiple lines at the exact same time to earn massive combo multipliers and skyrocket your high score.

4

Don't Get Stuck

Think ahead! The game is over when you run out of room and can no longer place any of the pieces from your tray.
