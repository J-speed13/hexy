MEDIEVAL CHURCH

Question 1 of 100

Which Christian denomination did almost everyone in medieval England belong to, and what language was the Bible written in?

Explanation contents...

Revision Clue

Think about the dominant Church based in Rome and the language used by educated priests across Europe during this era.

Exam Strategy Check

To achieve a Distinction (85%+) on your Year 7 history exam, make sure you can explain the cause and effect of historical milestones, rather than just knowing the dates. Study the structures of castles, the dispute between Crown and Altar (Becket), and the power shift caused by the Black Death.

Rule of Law

Magna Carta (1215) set the standard that even kings must follow the law.

Miasma Fallacy

Medieval people blamed "bad air" for the plague, but fleas and bacteria were the true killers.

Feudal Decay

The 1381 Peasants' Revolt failed at first, but lords could never return to old forced duties.
