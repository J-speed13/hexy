Note on the Lines

The notes sitting on the five lines of the staff are E, G, B, D, F. Keep them memorized with this fun phrase:

"Every Good Burger Deserves Fries"

Notes in the Spaces

The notes located inside the spaces between lines spell out the word:

F - A - C - E

Ledger Lines

Notes appearing above or below the standard staff lines utilize small helper lines called ledger lines. For example, Middle C sits on a line below the treble stave!
